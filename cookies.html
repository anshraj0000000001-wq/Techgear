Cookies Policy

Transparency is at the heart of TechGear. We use cookies to refine our services and provide you with a seamless digital experience.

A. What are Cookies?

Cookies are sophisticated small data files stored on your device. They act as a memory for the web, allowing us to recognize your preferences, improve performance, and understand how you interact with our technology ecosystem.

B. Types of Cookies

Analytical

Powered by Firebase, these metrics help us optimize our content delivery and user flow.

Affiliate

Temporary markers that ensure referral credit when you explore partner products through our links.

C. Managing Your Privacy

You remain in total control. Your browser settings allow you to audit, delete, or block cookies. Note that disabling certain cookies may restrict access to advanced site features.
